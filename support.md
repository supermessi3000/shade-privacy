## Common Issues

**App not scanning messages properly**
- Make sure you have an active internet connection
- Ensure the message or screenshot is clearly visible

**Scan limit reached**
- You may need to purchase additional scans or upgrade your plan

**Account or login issues**
- Try signing out and back in
- If the issue persists, contact support
