# Privacy Policy for GhostGuard

**Last updated:** February 2026

GhostGuard (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Shade mobile application.

## Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

- Email address (if you create an account)
- Name (optional, if provided)
- Messages, text, screenshots, or links submitted for scam analysis

Shade does **not** collect payment card details, passwords, or government identification numbers.

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- Analyze content for scam and phishing detection

Submitted content is processed automatically and is not sold or used for advertising.

## Data Sharing

Shade does **not** sell, rent, or trade personal data.

We may disclose information only if required by law or to protect the security of the app.
